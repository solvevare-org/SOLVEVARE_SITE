How do we choose a tech stack for the project?

We analyze your requirements and business goals. Then we evaluate software complexity, business logic, and additional features. We consider the project's peculiarities to determine functionality and tools. In medium and large projects, it's never one technology — it's a combination of multiple programming languages, frameworks, 3rd party integrations, servers, and more.

The pre-implementation stage is often underestimated, but it positively influences the project. It helps us identify areas for improvement that you might not notice.
